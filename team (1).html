Our Team

Chirag Doshi

Managing Partner

He heads the virtual CFO, forensic, IND AS / IFRS and system and processes implementation spectrum of the services offered by CFRA

He is a Chartered Accountant from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and a Certified Information System Auditor (CISA) from US.

He brings in an experience of more than 15 years in the field of International accounting and auditing (Statutory and Forensic) gained in Canada, Germany, Dubai, Singapore, Egypt, Philippines, India, London and many other nations. He has worked with a Big 4 CA firm, Large Indian CA firms and a Multinational fortune 500 company.

He is vast experience in the field of IFRS / IND AS implementation, compliance, training and advising clients reporting and system integration in various countries for companies in various sectors such as Media and Entertainment, Retails, Information Technology, Turnkey Projects, Engineering, Power, Telecommunications, Shipping and Real Estate sectors.

He has also done many implementation projects related to Operating and accounting manual (including carrying out Sarbanes Oxley reviews) and Implementation of Internal Financial Controls for various clients in the manufacturing, Power, shipping and financial sector.

He is also a Managing Committee member, Convenor of Accounting and Auditing and Core Committee member of Human Resource and Information Technology Committee of Bombay Chartered Accountants Society.

He is also an eminent speaker at various forums like:

  • Certified course on IFRS held by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)
  • IFRS workshops conducted by ICAI
  • Ramanujan Collage University of Delhi
  • WIRC forums
  • Chamber of Tax Consultants
  • Bombay Chartered Accountants Society
  • Institute of Cost & Works Accountants of India
  • Various other private forums.
